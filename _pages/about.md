I am a Senior Research Economist at the <a href="https://www.bde.es" target="_blank">Banco de España</a>.

My research studies the aggregate consequences — and computational aspects — of household and firm heterogeneity, with a focus on housing and mortgage markets and their interaction with monetary policy.
